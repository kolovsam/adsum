About Me

22-Straight Years A Student
HCDE PhD Student

I'm a sixth/end year PhD student at the University of Washington in the Human-Centered Design and Engineering (HCDE) department. I work with my advisors Kate Starbird and Sean Munson exploring the use of tracking technology (e.g., Catapult, WHOOP, Fitbit, etc.) and data in college athletics. In the 2020-2021 academic year I wrote and defended my dissertation proposal. I plan to finish my PhD in the 2021-2022 academic year and so I will be applying for academic and industry jobs this year!

UMass Amherst Computer Science & Rowing

In May 2016, I graduated from UMass Amherst with a BS in Computer Science from the College of Information and Computer Science (CICS). I also completed the Commonwealth Honors College program with an undergraduate capstone project. This project was a part of my work in the LASER Lab where I was advised by Lori A. Clarke. During my undergraduate career I also competed on the NCAA Divsion I UMass Women's Rowing Team. For details about my college rowing career, read my bio.
