UX for a Mobile Mental Health Intervention

I worked on this project as a research assistant in Dr. Pat Arean's CREATIV lab in UW Psychiatry and Behavioral Sciences. I conducted a study to get feedback on a mobile mental health app for long-term remote digital health studiies designed to deliver assessments and interventions to people experiencing symptoms of depression. This study aimed to improve the design of this app for future studies, and better understand how to keep participants engaged and understand their perspectives on passive sensing.
