Personal Data in College Athletics

Ongoing. I am leading this project with my advisors Sean Munson and Kate Starbird. We recently had our first paper on this project published. As part of this project, I ran three directed research groups (DRGs) in the 2017-2018 academic year. HCDE students and UW student-athletes worked together to design, conduct, and analyze athlete interviews exploring goals and concerns for collection and use of tracking data.
