I'm a third year PhD student at the University of Washington in the Human-Centered Design and Engineering (HCDE) department. Currently, I'm working with Kate Starbird, David McDonald, and Sean Munson to start a new project exploring the use of tracking technology (Catapult, WHOOP, Fitbit, etc.) and data in college athletics. In this 2018-2019 academic year I am finishing a UX study for a mental health app that I started over the summer and preparing to take my general exam in thespring.

I also completed the Commonwealth Honors College program with an undergraduate capstone project. This project was a part of my work in the LASER Lab where I was advised by Lori A. Clarke.
